EHMAN

The project EHMAN (Expanding the Horizons of Manufacturing: Solving the Integration Paradox) aims at the enterprise-wide modeling and optimization by the development and application of integrated modeling, simulation and optimization methodologies, and computer aided tools for reliable and sustainable improvement opportunities within the entire manufacturing network and its individual components.

EHMAN will build on it the new integrated Supply Chain Management paradigm handling external and internal dynamics of real businesses, and where the different toolboxes will share a common database and plug and play facilities. In addition, EHMAN will provide the industry with a significant competitive edge thanks to the enhanced SC dynamics and consequently to a radically improved way to handle an uncertain globalizing market by optimizing business production resources.

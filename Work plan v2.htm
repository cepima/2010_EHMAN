WORKPACKAGE 5

Real-time and proactive scheduling

Objective

To develop a modeling framework for real-time scheduling of in batch, and discrete operating modes in complex enterprises.

Description of the work

o   A methodology to model and solve scheduling problems under real times aspects within relatively short computation times and based upon uncertain data.

o   This modeling framework reflects the need for immediately applicable first stage decisions.

o   Robust production scheduling.

 

Milestones and expected results

o   A set of modeling tools and novel solution techniques for real-time production scheduling.

o   Technical and scientific research progress.

o   Satisfactory completion of all work plan items.

o   Existence of positive and realistic objectives for the industrial exploitation of the results.

 

 

WORKPACKAGE 6

Integrating the tracking system of the network dynamics

Objective

To develop a Model Predictive Control (MPC) framework within the holistic decision-making model to continuously improve the Supply Chain (SC) planning which will make it possible to integrate the three classical SC hierarchical decision levels: strategic decisions of capacity expansion, planning & scheduling and supervisory & basic control leading to a dynamically responsive SC to incidents at any of those levels, thus reflecting the external and internal dynamics of real businesses.

Description of the work

o   Production network control strategy.

o   Holistic decision-making framework design.

 

Milestones and expected results

o   A set of novel modeling tools and techniques for SC design and operation capturing its dynamics for enhanced production sustainability.

o   Technical and scientific research progress.

o   Satisfactory completion of all work plan items.
